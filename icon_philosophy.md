# Folded Clarity

A philosophy of quiet distillation — where a mass of raw input becomes a single confirmed point. The app icon is a document with a folded corner: proof that something was captured, read, and reduced. The fold marks the moment of intake; the lines mark the moment of compression; the check marks the moment of trust.

The mark reads left to right, top to bottom, exactly like the app works: material comes in (photo, recording, file, gallery), text is extracted, then narrowed into three lines of decreasing weight — many words becoming a few, becoming fewer. A single check badge closes the sequence, the same visual language used across the result and history screens, so the icon is not decoration but a preview of the product itself.

Color is restrained to two tones: a deep, quiet teal field and a plain white document. No gradient, no shadow, no typographic mark. The palette is deliberately unglamorous so the icon sits calmly among a home screen of louder apps, and so it still reads correctly at 40pt in a notification or a settings row.

Every other surface in the app — buttons, badges, section labels — reuses this same teal as the single accent color. The icon is not a one-off graphic; it is the seed the rest of the design system grows from.
